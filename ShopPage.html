lierre

This fast growing creeper that grows without anything is a true gift of nature. 'lierre' is therefore somewhat of an exception, being present in temperate climates. It does well in shade and sun, and in any type of soil.

Aglaonema

320 MAD

Aglaonema

Without doubt one of the best depolluting plants for our interiors. It is a robust tropical plant, easy to maintain. No need for a bright apartment, L’aglaonema requires little light to thrive. Like any tropical plant, 'Aglaonema' does not like cold or dry heat. Place it in partial shade without direct sunlight, away from drafts .

Aloe Vera

250 MAD

Aloe Vera

Once well installed in your living room, your office or bedroom, Aloe Vera is ready to make real small miracles to purify the ambient air and make it healthier and more breathable. Indeed, a single plant of Aloe Vera is able to absorb almost all the carbon monoxide of a room. And as if that were not enough, Aloe Vera is also able to reduce allergies.

Spathiphyllum

In our climates, this plant takes root inside our homes. This plant dreads direct sunlight but needs excellent light, so place it near a window making sure that it is not inundated by direct sunlight.
